East Sea Battle

Ten years ago, Xiang Yi Li , the master of the Sigu Sect, dominated with his superior swordsmanship and was a symbol of light in the martial arts world. However, he suddenly disappeared along with Fei Sheng Di, the leader of the Jinyuan Alliance, after they arranged to battle in the East Sea. The Sigu Sect was also disbanded.

Mysterious Lianhua

Ten years later, Lian Hua Li is a countryside doctor who travels dragging around a lotus tower. He accidentally gets pulled into the pugilistic world. Duo Bing Fang vows to find proof that Lian Hua has been impersonating a famous doctor. Meanwhile, Fei Sheng Di recognizes that the Lian Hua Li is none other than Xiang Yi Li.

Wonderful Detective

After a series of cases, Lian Hua Li, Fei Sheng Di and Duo Bing Fang gradually come to forge a deep friendship and join hands in solving mysterious cases to uphold justice. They finally find the Lianhua's Senior brother is not dead and is the mastermind behind the turmoil in the world.

Wonderful Cases

This series narrates a total of 10 bizarre cases, where Li Lianhua, as a detective, uses wisdom and courage to uncover the truth. The final case involves his last wish, which is to find and bury the remains of his Senior brother. As the investigation deepens, he discovers that his Senior brother didn't die and that his poisoning 10 years ago is also related to him. The plot is intricate and engaging. The 10 mysterious cases also allegorically reflect Li Lianhua's life experiences.

Life Attitude

A story about a hero descending from the pedestal, becoming an ordinary person, and eventually reconciling with himself and others. At a time when all cultural works are keen on telling the secrets of achieving success, this story goes against the tide by narrating a story about letting go of success. Li Lianhua gave up the title of the best in the world and gave us a surprising answer - a pure heart brings blossoms everywhere.

Touching Emotion

Compared to striving for the peak, it's more difficult to be a ordinary person. Once upon a time, Xiangyi danced with a sword on the rooftop to please his lover， but now he let his lover marries someone else, only wishing her happiness. Ten years ago, he was poisoned by a disciple and now he chooses to use his final moments to detoxify him. All past events are gone with yesterday. He discovers the truth, takes up his sword once again and seeks revenge for his master.

The life of Li Lianhua is riddled with regrets. His regrets manifest in various ways:

  • as a hero, he once wielded his sword in dance but fell into a decade of peril on the brink of life and death.
  • The experiences of his youth in the martial world are irretrievable, and his once arrogant demeanor remains irreparable.
  • Past relationships are hard to sever.
  • despite a reunion with his beloved,he has no choice but to sadly let go.
  • Former comrades recall his faults while forgetting his contributions.
  • He once shone in the martial world but failed to maintain his former glory.
  • Destiny predetermined his fate; his future is uncertain, his senses lost.

In the end, he cannot remain in the mortal realm; the youthful and reckless him repaid the martial world with his own life. These regrets weave together to form the epitome of Li Lianhua's life.
